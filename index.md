## Research & Publications

Her research activity focuses on mechanical manufacturing processes, with particular emphasis on process monitoring and machine-tool connectivity within the broader framework of Smart Manufacturing. She works primarily on machining, micromachining, and additive manufacturing, studying how advanced data acquisition and digital integration can improve process reliability and efficiency. Her applications of interest span the aerospace and petroleum industries, as well as the field of biocompatible materials for medical implants. The materials she commonly investigates include titanium and aluminum alloys, chrome-cobalt alloys, and aeronautical composite materials.
